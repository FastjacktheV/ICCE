    • Hoofdstuk 14. No detailed attention will be devoted to finite state automatons (FSAs) during the lectures, but FSAs lay at the heart of lexical scanners. In some situations it is arguably worth while to construct your own lexical scanner. Ch. 14 describes the background and construction of table-driven FSAs.
